What and Why Do We Need Asynchronous Stuff

Up until now, you have probably written some JavaScript code, press Run and BOOM : not a second later, some lines (or errors) are printed out onto the console. Great !

But what if you want to do something that takes a longer time to finish, for example downloading a cat image for your application of Cute Cats Collection ?

Since Javascript is a single-threaded programming language, meaning it can only do one thing at a time, so if you just fetch your image normally, the app will be lag and your users won't be able to interact with the rest of your app until that cat image has finished downloading. And if your app has to download multiple images, ONE BY ONE ? Oh boy.
Lucky for you, this is where asynchronous programming comes in.
