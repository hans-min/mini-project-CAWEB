What and Why Do We Need Asynchronous Stuff

Up until now, you have probably written some JavaScript code, press Run and BOOM : not a second later, some lines (or errors) are printed out onto the console. Great! But what if you want to do something that takes a longer time to finish, for example downloading a cat image for your application of Cute Cats Collection ? If you just fetch your image as normal, the users of your app won't be able to interact with your app until the image is finished downloading. This is where asynchronous programming comes in.
